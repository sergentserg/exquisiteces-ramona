Our Restaurant

Exquisiteces Ramona started as a family catering service within the owner's family, but with some effort it expanded into a full-blown restaurant that has been alive these past 3 years.

Our curated menu is a result of years of experimenting with some of our favorite recipes. You can call us a Hispanic fusion of sorts, but we don't like to put a label on it; we're open to serving any kind of food we happen to like, and that we think you'll like!
